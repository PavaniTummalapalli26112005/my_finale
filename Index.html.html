Our Courses

Aditya Degree College offers a variety of courses:

  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration): A comprehensive program that focuses on business management, entrepreneurship, and leadership skills.
  • B.Sc (Data Science): Dive into the world of big data, machine learning, and statistical analysis.
  • B.Sc (Computer Science): Learn the fundamentals of computer programming, algorithms, and software development.
  • BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications): A specialized course focusing on software development, networking, and IT infrastructure.
  • B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce): Gain expertise in accounting, finance, marketing, and business law.
